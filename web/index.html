BillRaja — Billing, Employee Attendance, Team Management & GST App for Small Businesses in India

BillRaja is the complete business management app built for Indian small businesses. From billing and invoicing to employee attendance tracking, team collaboration, membership management, and GST compliance — run your entire business from one app. Available on Android, iOS, and Web.

Billing & Invoicing

Create professional GST-compliant invoices in seconds. Choose from 20+ invoice templates. Share bills instantly via WhatsApp, SMS, email, or direct link. Collect payments faster with UPI QR codes embedded in every invoice. Track payment status — paid, pending, overdue — all at a glance.

Employee Attendance Tracking

Track employee attendance with QR code check-in and geo-location verification. Monitor daily attendance, view attendance reports, and manage staff schedules. Perfect for shops, offices, warehouses, and field teams. No separate attendance app needed.

Team Management & Collaboration

Invite team members, assign roles, and control access with role-based permissions. Manage who can create invoices, view reports, or handle customers. Built for business owners who need their staff to collaborate without giving away full control.

GST Compliance

Automatic GST calculations with CGST, SGST, and IGST support. Generate GST reports for filing. HSN code support for all products. Stay compliant without hiring an accountant. Built specifically for Indian tax requirements.

Customer & Client Management

Maintain a complete customer database. Track purchase history, outstanding balances, and payment patterns for every client. Organize customers into groups. Send payment reminders and balance notifications.

Inventory & Product Management

Manage your product catalog with pricing, HSN codes, and real-time stock tracking. Get low-stock alerts. Track product movements and stock history. Create purchase orders for suppliers.

Membership & Subscription Management

Manage member subscriptions, recurring plans, and membership renewals. Perfect for gyms, clubs, coaching centres, co-working spaces, and any subscription-based business.

Business Reports & Analytics

Real-time dashboards showing revenue trends, outstanding amounts, top customers, and business performance. GST reports, sales reports, and payment analytics — make data-driven decisions.
